QQ Music


Which makes life better


QQ Music is affiliated with China's online music service leader Tencent Music Entertainment Group and is a leading domestic music streaming platform. Since its establishment in 2005, the total number of QQ music registered users has reached 800 million. With high-quality content as the core and big data and Internet technology as the driving force, QQ Music is committed to creating a “smart” pan-music ecosystem with “sound” and providing users with a diversified music life experience. On November 8, 2019, QQ Music officially launched an open platform.
